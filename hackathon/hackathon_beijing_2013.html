2013年 北京 Eclipse编程马拉松(Eclipse Hackathon Beijing 2013)


时间和地点（When and where）

本次活动将和Eclipse Day（2013,北京）活动同时举行，详细的地点和时间可见于[http://eclipse-china.github.io/ 活动主页]

(English) You can visit the main page of [http://eclipse-china.github.io/ Eclipse Day Beijing] to get the detail informations.


主题（Theme）

不预设任何知识性要求。虽然，了解或熟悉相关Eclipse平台开发的相关领域，可能会玩的更高兴。我们希望参加此次Hackathon的朋友们，能够对使用或贡献到Eclipse有更多的心得或兴趣。这就足够了！

(English) No any specific knowledge of Eclipse or others is mandatory. You are very welcome even if you are one artist. Yes, we are just hacking for fun! It is great to see that we could contribute to any part of Eclipse. However, finally, it is best if we could say "Hi, guy, I like your plugin" or "Oh, it is amazing that we can use Eclipse like this"...
